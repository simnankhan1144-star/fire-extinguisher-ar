🖨️ Print Your AR Marker

Place this marker flat on a surface, then scan your QR code to start the AR tutorial.

Hiro Marker — Print at 100% scale (A4 or Letter)

📋 How to use:

  1. Tap Print Marker below (or ⌘P / Ctrl+P).
  2. Place the printed sheet on any flat surface — table, floor, or wall.
  3. Open the AR Tutorial via the QR code with your phone.
  4. Point your phone camera at this marker.
  5. The 3D fire extinguisher will appear — tap Next to learn each step!
💡 Tip: Good lighting works best. Keep the marker fully visible in the camera frame. The further away your phone, the more of the extinguisher you'll see!
